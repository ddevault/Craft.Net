PvP Support Introduced

13 September 2012

As of commit 54e1ae7bf1, you and your friends are able to hop on a Craft.Net server and duke it out! Damage is accurately calculated based on weapons used. However, armor is not factored into the calculation, mostly because it’s impossible to put on without support for survival-mode inventory management. But don’t worry - that’s coming in this iteration.

By Drew DeVault
